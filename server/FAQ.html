What is inBin?

inBin, short for "intelligentBin", was created by a group of 3rd Year engineering students from RMIT University in Melbourne, Australia for their year-long design project.

You can find out more about the inBin Project here.


Where can I download inBin?

inBin can be downloaded from the App Store on your compatible iPhone here.


What platforms does your app support?

At this time, only iOS (7.0+) is supported.
In addition, only iPhone devices compatible with Bluetooth 4.0 are supported. This includes:

  • iPhone 5, iPhone 5c
  • iPhone 5s
  • iPhone 6, iPhone 6 Plus
  • iPhone 6s, iPhone 6s Plus
  • iPhone SE
  • iPhone 7, iPhone 7 Plus


Why is there no Android or Windows Phone app?

Unfortunately we did not have enough time to develop both iOS and Android applications during the 10-week Telstra University Challenge.

An Android application is on the roadmap for a future release should inBin be continued beyond Proof of Concept status.

There are currently no plans to support Windows Phone (sorry).
